Bitcoin

Hello and welcome to Bitcoin!
For those who may not be familiar, Bitcoin is a decentralized digital currency that was created in 2009 by an unknown individual or group of individuals using the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto.

more It operates on a peer-to-peer network, meaning that transactions are conducted directly between users without the need for a central authority. One of the key features of Bitcoin is its limited supply. There will only ever be a total of 21 million Bitcoins that can be mined, and as of today, more than 18 million have already been mined. This limited supply, combined with increasing demand, has led to the volatility and speculation that have come to define the Bitcoin market. It's undeniable that Bitcoin will have a significant impact on the world and it will be interesting to see how it continues to evolve in the future.

scenario

People have become increasingly distrustful of their goverments and the fiat money they use. This distrust has been caused by a variety of factors, such as economic instability, inflation and goverment corruption. As a result people want to be more independent and looking for alternative form of money. The global economy has been hit by a major financial crisis. Goverments around the world have been unable to stabilize their currencies. In this situation, Bitcoin has become the go-to-currency. Its decentralized nature and lack of goverment control make it very attractive. The world will be more connected than before. Businesses will be able to accept payments from customers all over the world.
