Charts/Indicators

Charts are visual representations of data, typically used to depict the price movement of a security or asset over a specific period of time. In the context of Bitcoin, charts can be used to show the price of Bitcoin over time, either in terms of a specific currency (e.g. USD) or in terms of Bitcoin itself.
Indicators are statistical measures that are used to help analyze and interpret price data. Indicators are often overlaid on top of price charts, and can provide additional context and insight into the price movement of an asset.
