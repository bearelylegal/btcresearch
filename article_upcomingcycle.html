cycle - 2024-2027

Charts like the Network Utilization to Price Level (NUPL) and Stock to Flow (S2F) models can be used to predict future trends in the price of Bitcoin. The NUPL chart compares the amount of Bitcoin being used on the network (network utilization) to its current price, and can indicate whether the coin is undervalued or overvalued.
The Stock to Flow chart compares the existing supply of Bitcoin to the rate at which new coins are being added to the market, and can be used to predict long-term price trends.

moreWhile these charts can provide valuable insights, it's important to remember that they are based on historical data and cannot predict future events with certainty. Additionally, it is important to consider other factors that may affect the price of Bitcoin such as adoption, regulations, and overall market sentiment.
